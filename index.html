Welcome!

Why did you do Ironhack?

There is one thing that gives me more “stamina” than coffee: challenges. In this journey of solving problems my first step was looking for a human-centered methodology that included the importance of listening and provided the tools I needed to become the expert that I want to become. Therefore when I discovered UX/UI design I wanted to enjoy the ironhack bootcamp because it’s a method that fills the gap between problems/people needs and product/solutions. For personal reasons, this method is the net that link everything that fulfills me: people, tech, challenges, investigation, creativity and innovation.

What do you look for in a company culture?

In a company I want to feel that I'm part of a larger common goal where people are engaged to be part of a larger purpose. I look for a company culture that provides employees with a sense of safety and well-being and has a policy where everyone looks out for each other. For last, a place where employees feel their voices can be heard when they aren’t muffled under layers of management.

Superpower & why?

My superpower is empathy because it permits me to escape the limits of my own life and enter in the world of another human being, understanding the depth of their feelings and thoughts.
